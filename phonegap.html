what we does

We display photos in the gallery with full swipe capability , so viewers can navigate through the photos using the same swipe action they are familiar with.With this, you can add a description with each gallery , and a general introduction .you can create specific galleries for display in your mobile photo gallery.This allows you to be very selective about which photo should appear in these galleries. myGallery also offers quick access to sharing photo and also transfers photo to social media and from your camera to your computer with a simple import process and allows use to create multimedia slide shows.
